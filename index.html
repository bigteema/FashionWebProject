Welcome to My Archival Fashion Space

Hi, I’m Tima, a senior at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Economics. I love archival fashion, sourcing, reselling, and authenticating vintage designer pieces. I study how construction, materials, and design details tell stories about their eras. This site mixes my academic side with my creative side.

You’ll find an Archive Guide about authenticating Chanel, Balenciaga, and Jean Paul Gaultier, plus a Curations page with my favorite runway pieces and playlists I made to match the vibe. My goal is to show how data, design, and culture overlap.
