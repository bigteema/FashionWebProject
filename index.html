Welcome to My Archival Fashion Space

Hi, I’m Tima — a senior at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Economics. I’m passionate about archival fashion, sourcing, reselling, and authenticating vintage designer pieces. I love exploring the history of design through construction, textiles, and detail work. This site blends my academic projects with my personal love for fashion and creative expression.

You’ll find an archive guide where I break down how I authenticate brands like Chanel, Balenciaga, and Jean Paul Gaultier, a curation page featuring favorite finds with sources, and a few of my playlists that capture the mood behind my sourcing adventures. My goal is to show how data, creativity, and culture intersect — whether through analytics or aesthetics.
