Archival Fashion Authentication Guide

How I check and verify vintage pieces

This is how I research and evaluate archival items. I usually start by studying references like lookbooks or runway shots from the same era. Then I compare construction details, materials, and tags. I focus on accuracy of fonts, stitching, hardware engravings, and the type of zippers used. I use multiple sources and always double check authenticity signs before buying or selling.

Quick checklist

  • Runway or lookbook match for the design
  • Correct label placement and fonts
  • Hardware consistency with era
  • Stitch spacing and backstitch security
  • Clear seller photos and provenance

Brand notes

  • Chanel: turnlock engraving depth and quilting alignment
  • Balenciaga: zipper brand and tag font from the right year
  • Jean Paul Gaultier: mesh print quality and tag style
